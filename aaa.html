About CHAYAMANA

CHAYAMANA is the flagship annual photography exhibition and competition organized by the Mahanama College Photographic Art Society (MCPAS). It provides a platform for young photographers across Sri Lanka to showcase their creativity, share unique perspectives, and celebrate the art of photography while promoting artistic excellence, technical skill, and innovation.

History
OF CHAYAMANA

Since its inception, CHAYAMANA has evolved from a school photography exhibition into one of Sri Lanka's respected inter-school photography events. Organized annually by the Mahanama College Photographic Art Society (MCPAS), the exhibition has provided countless young photographers with the opportunity to display their work, receive professional recognition, and develop their artistic vision. Each edition reflects the dedication of the organizing committee, the passion of participating photographers, and the continued growth of photographic art within the school community. While embracing modern techniques and evolving styles, CHAYAMANA remains committed to its founding purpose—promoting creativity, encouraging excellence, and inspiring a lifelong appreciation for photography.

Collaboration is at the heart of what we do. Our team thrives on the synergy that arises when unique perspectives converge, fostering an environment of boundless creativity. By harnessing our collective expertise, we produce extraordinary results that consistently surpass expectations.
